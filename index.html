FAQs

Do I have ownership of the property?
Absolutely Yes! As a shareholder of the SPV that owns the asset, you have co-ownership of the entire asset.
How many nights of free stay do I get? How can I book the unit?
Each fraction owner gets 3 days of free stay at the property annually. You can book additional nights at a discounted rate. You can reach out to your dedicated relationship manager and book the dates of stay. Please note, the property bookings are one first come first stay basis.
Can I get full ownership of the house?
Yes, we do provide full and direct ownership of the house. In this case, we will not be forming an SPV and the property ownership can be registered on an individual. In this case, each property owner is eligible to get 30 days of free stay at the property annually.
Who will manage the property?
Relax and unwind knowing your vacation home is in expert hands! StayVista, a leading vacation rental company in India with over 920 properties under their care, will handle professional management. This ensures optimal rental income for you when you're not enjoying your getaway, and gives you peace of mind knowing your property is well- maintained by industry leaders.
How is the concept different from Timeshare?
Finplify does not offer timeshare. You will be co-owning the asset which has potential to appreciate over time and reap the benefits of appreciation.
What exactly is fractional ownership at Finplify?
Each property is owned by a special purpose vehicle(SPV) and this SPV can be co-owned by 10 people. The income generated by the property is proportionately distributed among the co-owners and each co-owner is eligible to stay upto 3 days per year.
